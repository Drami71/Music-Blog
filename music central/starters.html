Starters Guide

Interested in trying out some of these genres? I've picked out some albums
from metal, rock and hardcore that should serve as a good starting point.

The Beatles-Abbey Road Arguably one of the greatest rock albums of all time, the fun melodies will keep you entertained from start to end.
Iron Maiden-The Number of the Beast A cornerstone of heavy metal, you can't go wrong with this classic.
Converge-All We Love We Leave Behind This hardcore album holds no punches. Be prepared for a sonic onslaught.
My Bloody Valentine-Loveless A cornerstone of the shoegazing genre, don't let the distortion frighten you. A great album to lose yourself to.
Protest the Hero-Kezia A great album to get into prog-metal. Technicality and a vocal performance to remember.
Touché Amoré-Parting the Sea Between Brightness and Me A short, emotional album that is easily digestible. You can't help to fall for Bolm's personal lyrics.
